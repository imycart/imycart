<div class="blog-content">
   <p>There are plenty of hair brushes sold on the market which you can just bring back home. Why not make an ordinary hair brush into a nice one with a nice lace bow by doing some DIY?</p>
   <p>Actually doing handwork is more about attitudes, you will have a pair of clever hands and good senses if you have the right attitude and love for life. Exquisite and beautiful small handcrafts bring the feelings of freshness and simplicity. By some casual patching and easy steps many beautiful handicrafts can be made. Who doesn’t like this?
   </p><p>Today our fabric art lesson is about a pretty little thing which is quite simple, we will learn how to make a girls’ hair brush with ribbons, with very normally seen materials such as laces and ribbons, together with some glue and a normal metal hair brush. A bow can be made from a simple knot, and by gluing some layers together a pink and delicate flower is done. You must look pretty and charming wearing it.
   </p><p>Materials: a normal black hair brush, ribbons, laces, plush balls, non woven Fabric, scissors, glue.</p>
	<b>Steps</b>
	<ul>
	<li>Glue the plush ball onto one side of the ribbon and glue a layer of non woven fabric on the other side of the ribbon to add some thickness.</li>
	<li>Cut down some laces and fold them into a half flower like shown in the picture.</li>
	<li>Tie a bow with other half of the ribbon and glue it under the half flower.</li>
	<li>Glue all the above decorations onto the brush, then a beautiful hair brush is done!</li>
	</ul>
